I defended my PhD dissertation titled "New Messengers & New Physics: A Survey of the High-energy Universe" on April 24, 2023, at the University of Maryland, College Park. My committee consisted of Dr. Regina Caputo (Chair), Prof. Massimo Ricotti, Prof. Cole Miller, Prof. Peter Shawhan, and Dr. Christopher Karwin. I will post the full thesis upon finalizing the revisions---until then, please enjoy the slides from the public portion of my defense as well as some celebratory photos!
